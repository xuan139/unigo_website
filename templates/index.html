Get Started

Top 3 Features

Plug & Play

Connect the SSD to your Mac and launch without any setup or installation.

Gaming Ready

Preloaded with Steam, Epic, runtime libraries, and for most AAA titles.

VM Support

Comes with VMware virtual machines so you can launch Windows 11 instantly or DIY Ubuntu or others.

Support for Mac

OStation is optimized for MacOS, offering seamless performance across systems so you can play anywhere.

  • Supports USB-C and Thunderbolt
  • Compatible with macOS Catalina to Sonoma
  • Works with M1/M2/M3

Installation Guide

Step 1: Plug in the SSD

Connect the SSD to your Mac device via USB-C or Thunderbolt.

Step 2: Launch Toolkit

Open the OStation app to initialize game or virtual machine launch.

Step 3: Start Gaming

Enjoy Steam, Epic, or pre-configured games instantly. No setup needed.
